天氣安排

Rookie Academy has the weather policy in place to ensure the safety of children and their familty, and also to ensure our staff can safely travel to and from our centre.

If Tropical Cyclone Signal No. 3 or below, Amber or Red Rainstorm Warning Signal is hoisted, classes will continue as usual. If the signal No. 8 or above and/or the black rainstorm warning signal is still hoisted 2 hours before the start time, the center will be closed. If the typhoon warning is changed to Typhoon No. 3 after 11:00 in the morning, the center will be closed for the whole day. In any case, participants and parents should consider the weather, road and traffic conditions in each area respectively to decide whether to go to the center to participate in various activities.

Absence from classes and activities under such circumstances are not eligible for a refund or a reschedule of lessons.

If the No. 8 signal and/or the black rainstorm warning signal is lowered, the center will resume business and classes 2 hours after the signal is lowered.
