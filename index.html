About RookieAcademy

"Accompanying and loving our children will develop their talents."

Rookie Academy (RA) is located in Tuen Mun and Kwun Tong. Our team consists largely of pofessionals and teachers with extensive experience working with children with special educational needs. Our main audience are from pre-nursery children to the elder people. We provide assessment and consultation services, sensory integration therapies, interventions on behavioral and emotional development ,speech therapies and intensive pre-school children training.

Our motto is " Accompanying and loving our children will develop their talents".

Our Core Values

  • Sensory Development - maturing of the five senses
  • "Gross Motor" and "Fine Motor"

Our Mission

"The key to sucess is lifelong learning."

We are proponent of promoting self-directed learning abilities.

Self-directed learning is a process of learning requires students to take personal responsibility for their own learning. In Rookie Academy, our children will participate different kinds of courses to enhance their self-learning skills. To foster your child’s intrinsic motivation to grow using the power of parenting and connections.

Our Services

"Student first" has always been our teaching philosophy"

We are committed to provide high-quality teaching services and robust test-taking strategies.

We provide diversified training sessions to help students with special needs overcome their learning difficulties, so they can change from being difficult to engage in learning, to gradually enjoy learning, then to self-directed learning, and even to apply their knowledge.
